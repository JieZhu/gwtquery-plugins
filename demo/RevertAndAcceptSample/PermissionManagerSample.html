This example shows how to implement drag and drop in a CellTree by using a DragAndDropCellTree. Just add a member to the project by dragging and dropping it in the desired permission. You can also use drag and drop to reorganize the member's permissions, and even and remove them from your project by dropping them back on the "other members" list.
