Play checkers simply by dragging and dropping the pieces into the desired square. The game follows the rules of the US version of checkers. The pieces can only move forward and kings may move both forward and backward. When multiple jumps are possible, the player have one second between each jump to realize it. No artificial intelligence is implemented, you have to play for the two players alternatively
